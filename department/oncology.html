The Department of Oncology integrates health care personnel & diagnostic facilities in an environment dedicated to the care of Cancer patients of both adult & Pediatrics.


We have an international standard Oncology center with experienced & trained physicians and expert nurses along with all modern equipment for investigation facilities for early diagnosis of cancer and comprehensive cancer treatment. This center has state-of-the-art chemotherapy unit, so unified management of cancer is available at Bangladesh Specialized Hospital PLC.

Our Facilities


1. Outpatient department service: Experience Oncologists are available for consultancy, advice & counselling:
  • Radiation Oncologist.
  • Medical oncologist.
  • Pediatric Hemato Oncologist.
  • Hematologist
  • Surgical Oncologist
  • Gynecological Oncologist
  • Head & Neck Surgical Oncologist
  • Oral & Maxillofacial Surgical Oncologist
  • Neurosurgical Oncologist
2. Inpatient department service:
  • Management of cancer patients.
  • Complications of chemotherapy
  • Care of terminally ill patient
  • Pain management
  • Surgical oncology
  • Gynecological oncology
  • Palliative care
  • Head & Neck Surgical Oncologist
  • Oral & Maxillofacial Surgical Oncologist
  • Neurosurgical Oncologist
3. Chemotherapy unit:
  • Administration of regular chemotherapy.
  • Administration of Targeted therapy (Monoclonal Antibody).
  • Administration through and care of implanted venous access device (Port-A-Cath).
  • Pediatric chemotherapy
  • Intra-thecal administration of chemotherapy
  • Blood / Platelet transfusion
  • Bio-safety cabinet for standard drug mixing and handling
4. Counselling about chemotherapy, advice for terminally ill patient, end of life care.
5. Cancer screening: Comprehensive cancer screening packages for both Male & Female patients.
6. Nutrition consultation available round the clock.
7. Provide information and organize seminars about cancer.
8. Radiotherapy: Radiation is facilitated with the collaboration of Ahsania Mission Cancer & General Hospital, Uttara – 10, Dhaka.
