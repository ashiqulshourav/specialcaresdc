Associate Consultant (Microbiology) under Laboratory Medicine.

Responsibilities & Context

Job Summary:-

Bangladesh Specialized Hospital (PLC) is a leading healthcare provider in Bangladesh, known for its high standards of medical care, advanced technology, and commitment to patient well-being. We are seeking an Associate Consultant (Microbiology) with strong research and analytical skills to contribute to the hospital's microbiology department. The ideal candidate will be responsible for performing laboratory tests, analyzing patient specimens, interpreting results, ensuring infection control, and collaborating with other healthcare professionals to provide exceptional care.

Key Duties and Responsibilities:-

  • Laboratory Testing: Perform routine and complex microbiological tests, including bacteriology, virology, and mycology, on clinical samples.
  • Sample Analysis: Analyze specimens to identify microorganisms, assess their pathogenic potential, and determine antibiotic sensitivities.
  • Result Interpretation: Provide accurate reports to clinicians for the diagnosis and treatment of infections.
  • Quality Control: Ensure test accuracy through stringent quality control procedures and participation in quality assurance programs.
  • Infection Control: Monitor antimicrobial resistance, investigate outbreaks, and implement preventive measures.
  • Research: Conduct research on microorganisms and develop new diagnostic and therapeutic tools.
  • Collaboration: Work closely with doctors, nurses, and pharmacists to develop and implement infection control protocols.
  • Safety: Maintain a safe laboratory environment, following safety protocols for handling infectious materials.
  • Documentation: Keep accurate records of tests, analyses, and research findings.
  • Education and Training: Provide training and guidance to healthcare professionals on infection control.
  • Supervision: Supervise and train laboratory staff.
  • Equipment Maintenance: Ensure proper calibration and maintenance of laboratory equipment.
  • Others: Manage purchase orders and monitor laboratory inventory.
